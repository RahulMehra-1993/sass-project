Chronic diseases

Ayurveda teaches that many chronic diseases are caused by an imbalance in the body. An Ayurvedic diet is designed to restore balance to the body and reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, stroke, cancer, and diabetes.

Immunity

Ayurveda teaches that a strong immune system is essential for overall health. An Ayurvedic diet is designed to support the immune system by including foods that are rich in antioxidants and other nutrients that boost immunity

Energy levels

Ayurveda teaches that food is the source of our energy. An Ayurvedic diet is designed to provide the body with the nutrients it needs to produce energy efficiently.

Digestion

Ayurveda places a strong emphasis on digestive health. An Ayurvedic diet is designed to promote healthy digestion by including foods that are easy to digest and avoiding foods that are difficult to digest.
